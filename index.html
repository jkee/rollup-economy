How evidence becomes a research-priority tier

The v5.1 campaign keeps the frozen H/F/C/D formulas and cuts across all 1,012 six-digit NAICS industries. A code-owned sample receives isolated source review; unsampled records remain visibly not reviewed. Missing evidence never becomes a low score.

The aggregate

A = (H + F + C + D) / 4 · L = min(H,F,C,D)

A rewards broad opportunity while L preserves the weakest dimension. Tiers use both, and the finite uncertainty envelope shows which tier changes remain plausible.

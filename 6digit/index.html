How evidence becomes a research-priority tier

The v5 campaign keeps the frozen H/F/C/D formulas and cuts, but replaces adapter inputs with fresh, industry-specific research packets and isolated source review. Missing evidence never becomes a low score.

Tier + interval→A + L→4 factors→Primitives + ranges→Rationale + bridges→Sources + caveats→Validator review→Change evidence

The aggregate

A = (H + F + C + D) / 4 · L = min(H,F,C,D)

A rewards broad opportunity while L preserves the weakest dimension. Tiers use both, and the finite uncertainty envelope shows which tier changes remain plausible.

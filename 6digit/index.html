How the evidence becomes a verdict

The model, factor meanings, formulas, thresholds, gates, and golden membership were frozen before this campaign. Phase 5 changes presentation only.

Verdict→Geometric mean→5 factors→Deterministic formulas→Inputs→Sources + caveats

The aggregate

S = (V × C × A × B × M)⅕

The geometric mean keeps every 0–10 factor consequential. A single weak factor cannot disappear inside an additive average.

Absolute verdict cuts

03

Hard constraints

KILL floor: any factor below 1.0.

Hell-Yes gate: every factor ≥ 6.0, DURABLE terminal demand, and at least MED confidence.

Caps: LOW confidence caps at Conditional; terminal pressure can also cap the result.

04

Publication policy

Publishable and publishable with caveats enter the product. Reject and invalid remain in the exclusions ledger.

05

Estimate policy

Judgment is allowed only when its method, rationale, plausible range, uncertainty, and source IDs are visible. ESTIMATE is not disguised as observation.

06

Canonical outputs

The page reads the final site dataset, closed campaign report, and golden analysis directly. Raw packets, records, memos, and reviews stay linked per result.

## Business-model lens
- Included: The industry activity assessed for task and demand context is government administration and operation of spaceflight, space research, exploration, and space-flight centers; under the fixed LMM outsourced-service lens, no establishment qualifies.
- Excluded: Private aerospace research and development, commercial space transportation, spacecraft and satellite manufacturing, government manufacturing establishments, defense activities classified elsewhere, nonprofit research institutions, financing vehicles, and entities outside the EBITDA band.
- Customer and revenue model: Government agencies and flight centers execute appropriated public missions and procure substantial contractor support rather than sell recurring services to arm's-length customers for commercial revenue; private aerospace R&D is classified outside 927110.
- Deviation from default lens: none

## Executive view
The code is structurally outside the commercial roll-up lens: Census reserves it for government spaceflight, research, exploration, and flight centers and classifies private aerospace R&D elsewhere. AI exposure is meaningful, but there is no qualifying firm population or commercial retention construct.

## How AI changes the work
Likely uses include scientific and mission-data analysis, coding, simulation assistance, document and requirements drafting, knowledge retrieval, planning, anomaly triage, and program administration. Physical integration, testing, launch operations, independent verification, safety certification, and accountable scientific and engineering decisions remain human-led.

## Value capture
Government may convert AI gains into faster science, larger mission scope, avoided cost, or improved oversight, but these are public outputs rather than EBITDA retained through customer pricing. Contractor economics sit outside this NAICS code.

## Firm availability
Eligibility is observed at zero because 927110 is defined for government establishments and private aerospace R&D belongs to 541715. Agency reorganizations and procurement recompetes are not transferable-company control events.

## Demand durability

## Risks and uncertainty

Additional uncertainties include absent l and n, mission and budget volatility, scientific error, hallucination, reproducibility, export controls, classified data, cyber risk, legacy systems, procurement friction, flight safety, contractor dependence, and commercialization.

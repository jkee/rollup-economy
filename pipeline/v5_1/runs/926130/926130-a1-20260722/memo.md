## Business-model lens
- Included: No qualifying lower-middle-market firms were identified inside this government-only NAICS classification; public utility commissions and government communications, electric, gas, water, and sewerage regulatory offices are retained only as operational analogues for workflow research.
- Excluded: Government regulatory bodies as captive public units, utility operators classified in Sector 22, private regulatory consultants and inspectors classified elsewhere, shells, and non-control financing vehicles.
- Customer and revenue model: Government offices administer, regulate, license, and inspect utilities through appropriations, assessments, or statutory fees rather than recurring outsourced-service contracts sold by a transferable firm to external customers.
- Deviation from default lens: none

## Executive view
Utility regulation has a credible AI workflow opportunity and durable demand, but 926130 is government-only and therefore contains no qualifying transferable LMM firms. Regulators process structured filings, analyze markets, draft orders, inspect facilities, and enforce complex rules; AI can improve the information layer, while sovereign authority and public accountability remain with the agency.

## How AI changes the work
Likely applications include filing intake, data-quality checks, tariff and precedent research, public-comment synthesis, anomaly explanation, investigation triage, inspection planning, and draft reports or orders. FERC already uses automated validations and surveillance, so those benefits are not counted again. Engineering review, field inspection, contested hearings, enforcement judgment, and final decisions remain human-led.

## Value capture
There is no commercial value-capture construct inside this NAICS code. An agency may convert savings into lower fees, reduced appropriations, faster case processing, or additional oversight, but none is retained gross benefit under a customer contract subject to renewal pricing and competition.

## Firm availability
Census defines the industry as government establishments and explicitly places operating utilities elsewhere. Private consultants, inspectors, engineers, and software vendors may serve commissions, but they are outside 926130. Public appointments, reorganizations, or jurisdiction transfers are not qualifying control transactions.

## Demand durability
Communications, electric, gas, water, and sewer systems remain essential regulated infrastructure. Electricity demand, generation capacity, data centers, distributed resources, pipelines, spectrum use, reliability, cybersecurity, and public participation can add complexity and workload even when automated filing and monitoring reduce effort per item.

## Risks and uncertainty
The decisive weakness is an empty commercial lens population. Operationally, AI adoption also faces legacy systems, confidential infrastructure and market data, administrative-record requirements, explainability, procurement, uneven state resources, and liability for missed violations or erroneous orders.

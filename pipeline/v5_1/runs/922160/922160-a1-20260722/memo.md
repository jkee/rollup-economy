**Weakness:** Official NAICS boundaries leave no transferable LMM firm or commercial value-capture mechanism inside 922160.

## Business-model lens
- Included: The industry activity assessed for task and demand context is government firefighting, fire prevention, and combined fire-rescue or ambulance response; under the fixed LMM outsourced-service lens, no establishment qualifies.
- Excluded: Commercial firefighting, forest firefighting, private fire-alarm and sprinkler services, ambulance-only providers, government agencies without transferable equity, volunteer associations that are not transferable operating firms, and all entities outside the EBITDA band.
- Customer and revenue model: Government departments deliver tax- and appropriation-funded public safety rather than recurring services to arm's-length customers for commercial revenue; commercial firefighting is classified outside 922160.
- Deviation from default lens: none

## Executive view
The code is structurally outside the commercial roll-up lens: Census defines it as government fire-protection establishments and assigns commercial firefighting elsewhere. AI can improve public-department data and administration, but there is no qualifying firm population or commercial benefit-retention construct.

## How AI changes the work
Likely uses include incident-report drafting, NERIS data integration, pre-incident planning, inspection and prevention documentation, risk analytics, scheduling, training administration, inventory, and alert triage. Suppression, rescue, emergency medicine, driving, maintenance, and scene command remain human and physical.

## Value capture
Public agencies may convert productivity into readiness, service quality, avoided overtime, or budget savings, but those outcomes are not EBITDA retained through customer pricing. Commercial retention is therefore missing under the required lens.

## Firm availability
Eligibility is observed at zero because 922160 is defined for government establishments and commercial firefighting belongs to 561990. Government reorganizations and procurement changes are not transferable-company control events.

## Demand durability
Demand is durable and likely grows modestly because departments provide EMS, rescue, prevention, hazardous-material, disaster, and fire response. Incident mix, climate, demographics, budgets, prevention, and local service design widen the range.

## Risks and uncertainty
The decisive limitation is classification, not operational quality. Additional uncertainties include absent l and n, incomplete incident reporting, local fiscal stress, volunteer capacity, interoperability, medical-data privacy, cybersecurity, false alerts, liability, and disaster volatility.

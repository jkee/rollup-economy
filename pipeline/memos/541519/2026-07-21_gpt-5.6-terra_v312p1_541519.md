## Executive view

This residual code is heterogeneous: MSP-adjacent installation/support and resale-blended shops appear dominant, with disaster recovery and specialist deployment smaller. Automation can lower ticketing and configuration cost, but platform vendors and zero-touch deployment threaten the project revenue base.

## How AI changes the work

AI and automation affect triage, documentation, configuration scripts, monitoring and routine support. Field deployment, hardware handling, recovery orchestration, security judgment and client escalation remain human work, so the input is task compression rather than full technician replacement.

## Value capture

A recurring managed-service or DR contract can retain some efficiency, whereas project, incident and resale-plus-services books face competitive pass-through. Vendor platforms are packaging automation upstream; a small provider must own the account and a contracted outcome to keep savings.

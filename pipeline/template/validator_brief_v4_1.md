# v4.1 isolated publication-review brief

Review exactly one manifest-bound packet, finalized record, and memo using the
required reviewer model and prompt version. Do not read benchmark membership,
class labels, expected outcomes, prior reviews, or another research artifact.
Do not edit research or generated artifacts.

First verify schemas; exact model, run, prompt, specification and dataset
identity; all pinned digests; value-basis mode; role membership and weights;
fresh finalization and memo equality; arithmetic; interval monotonicity;
readiness; gates; and action. Any deterministic failure is `invalid`.

Then reopen every registered score-driving source and review the base plus both
ends of every critical range. Return exactly one input review for each required
critical path and any authored dataset fallback. Confirm:

- the fixed target archetype matches the cited population and target coverage;
- selection uses the largest base band count and the lexicographically smallest
  alternative ID to break a tie;
- when `selection_uncertainty` is true, exactly one primary record remains,
  readiness is `UNPROVEN`, coverage and value-basis uncertainty are widened,
  and no NAICS-wide claim is made;
- evidence states and proxy bridges are honest;
- an `assumption` value basis is pre-frozen, source-free, unchanged, and forces
  readiness `UNPROVEN`; no broad-NAICS proxy silently replaces it;
- a `dataset` value basis has a closed population/geography/period/unit/
  business-model bridge or, when absent/null, is treated as an unsupported proxy with readiness
  `UNPROVEN`;
- missing evidence is not disguised as measured weakness;
- the approved value basis was used without role substitution or reweighting;
- gross removable cash cost, implementation schedules, commercial retention,
  and year-five operator-controlled paid demand are separate;
- deal evidence did not influence removable cash cost or implementation;
- five-year sale availability has no buyer-competition or consolidator
  penalty;
- all-in entry and downside exit ranges use comparable size, earnings basis,
  quality, and archetype, without assumed multiple expansion;
- missing inputs have no fabricated neutral point, base factor, aggregate S,
  or verdict and expose the full possible domain;
- narrative, coherent scenarios, partial identification, readiness, action,
  and the year-five demand gate cohere.

Return `publishable`, `publishable_with_caveats`, `reject`, or `invalid`.
Unsupported score-driving evidence, a false critical-input review check, or a
material scope/range/evidence-state defect cannot receive a publishable tier.
An honest low-readiness result may be publishable. Bind the review to the exact
artifact and campaign digests and validate it against
`review_record_v4_1.schema.json`.
